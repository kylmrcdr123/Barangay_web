ABOUT US

Barangay Patutong Malaki North is one of the peaceful, progressive, and highly-urbanized community in Tagaytay City. Consists of cooperative and morally upright residents, ambitious and responsive business owners and corporate managers, and committed and visionary leaders, Barangay Patutong Malaki North works hand in hand with constituents to sustain its clean, green, safe, and healthy environment and to develop its physical and economic growth.
